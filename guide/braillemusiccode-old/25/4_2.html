Music Braille Code (2015)

25. Bowed String Instruments

25.4. Fingerings

25.4.2. Alternative Fingerings

Since the signs used to show lines of continuation in string music are the same signs as are used to show alternative fingerings in keyboard music, alternative fingerings in string music may not be shown with this device. Alternative fingerings in string music must be shown either by employing in-accords or as a variant. (See Pars. 21.5.2 and 21.5.3.)
