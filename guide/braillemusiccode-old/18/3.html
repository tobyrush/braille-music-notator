18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.3. Part-Measure Repeats

Subject to the general procedures described in Pars. 18.1â€“18.1.7, the braille repeat sign, along with any attached preceding or following signs, may be used to represent part of a measure when:

  1. the second half of the measure is an exact repetition of the first half,
  2. a complete beat is repeated immediately within the measure,
  3. a natural division of a beat is repeated immediately within the measure, or
  4. a chord written with interval signs is repeated immediately within the measure.
