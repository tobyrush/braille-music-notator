Music Braille Code (2015)

18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.2. Full-Measure Repeats

18.2.3. Precluded Full-Measure Repeats

The braille repeat sign may not be used to represent a full measure when:

  1. the repeated measure is at the beginning of a new segment, section, or braille page; or
  2. the notes of successive measures are the same, but the meters are different, as, for instance, six eighth notes in 3/4 meter followed by the same notes in 6/8 meter.
