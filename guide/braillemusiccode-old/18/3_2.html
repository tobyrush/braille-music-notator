Music Braille Code (2015)

18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.3. Part-Measure Repeats

18.3.2. Successive Part-Measure Repeats of Different Values

When a part-measure passage that ends with a repetition is immediately repeated in its entirety, the repeat sign representing the smaller value is immediately followed by dot 3 as a separator. A second repeat sign, along with any necessary octave marks or other modifiers, then follows to represent the larger pattern. The amount of music to be included in the larger pattern must be made obvious by the number of repeat signs given. If there is any doubt, the device should not be employed. This procedure may not be further extended to include more than two levels of rhythmic content.
