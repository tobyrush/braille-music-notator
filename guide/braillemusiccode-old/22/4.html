Music Braille Code (2015)

22. Nuances and Verbal Expressions

22.4. Fan-Shaped Beams

When the ligatures or beams of a rhythmic group are feathered (fan-shaped) rather than parallel, the notes of the group are to be executed as an accelerando or ritardando. Standard note values are used, but the group is preceded by the sign for accelerando ^<1,<1 or ritardando ,<1^<1 and is followed by the termination sign (dots 56, 13). If the ligatures start together and fan outward on succeeding notes, an accelerando is indicated; if the feathering is reversed, a ritardando is indicated.
