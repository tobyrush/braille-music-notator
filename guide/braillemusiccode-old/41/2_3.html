41.2. Vertical Score

41.2.3. Disposition of Hand Parts

When, as in organ accompaniments, hand and foot signs are used, they are not re-marked in every chord so long as the number of notes assigned to each in the first chord remains the same. When changes of disposition occur, these signs are only used as required to show the new disposition.
