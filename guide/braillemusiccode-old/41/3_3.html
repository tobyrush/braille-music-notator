Music Braille Code (2015)

41. Vertical Score and Bar-by-Bar

41.3. Bar-by-Bar

41.3.3. Marking Order of Parts

The order of the parts is shown by hand, foot or other signs, such as >VOICE' etc.These are placed at the commencement of the piece and are not re-marked in the second and succeeding measures unless a change of disposition occurs. Where one of the parts is omitted, or where the music of one part can be placed more conveniently with that for another, the hand signs are marked in such a way as to make the disposition of the music quite clear.
