Music Braille Code (2015)

40. Section-by-Section

40.1. Format of a Section

40.1.2. Measure Numbers Shown in Print

If measures are numbered in the print text, the numbers (written in the upper part of the cell without numeric indicator) must be placed between the measures with a blank space on either side. The first measure in a section does not require a number, since this number is given in the heading of the section.
