Music Braille Code (2015)

32. Accordions

32.1. Accordion Notation

32.1.6. True Melodic Bass

When there is a change from normal chord accordion to melody bass, the left-hand sign _> is inserted, the letters BAR, or other print indications are included, and octave signs are used. The button-row notation sign ,> is reintroduced at the return of chord accordion notation with row signs.
