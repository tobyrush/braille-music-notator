Music Braille Code (2015)

4. Clef Signs

4.1. Clef Signs Limited to Facsimile Transcriptions

Clef signs are routinely omitted in braille music transcription. However, when facsimile copy may be desirable, for instance in music transcribed for the benefit of the blind teacher with sighted pupils, the clefs used in the print must be marked in the braille copy.
