Music Braille Code (2015)

29. Keyboard Instruments

29.11. Piano Pedaling and Repeats

29.11.5. Original Pedaled and Repeat Not Pedaled

When a complete measure is pedaled and the following measure is a repeat without pedal, the full-measure repeat sign may be used; the pedal-up sign is brailled before the repeat sign, after the space separating the measures. In the case of a part- measure passage pedaled in the original and not pedaled in the repeat, the repeat sign is not used, and the repetition is written out.
