Music Braille Code (2015)

29. Keyboard Instruments

29.11. Piano Pedaling and Repeats

29.11.1. Pedaling Contained within Original and Repetition

The braille repeat may be used when the paired pedal-down and pedal-up apply only to part of the original and the repetition is pedaled identically. However, when the original measure begins with a pedal-down, and there are one or more further pedal indications in the measure, the full-measure repeat must not be used with a pedal-down indication preceding the repeat sign, because that pedal-down indication would be perceived to apply to the entire measure. If, in the print, both such identical measures begin with pedal-down and end with pedal-up, and the following measure begins with pedal-down, the measure repeat may be used without added pedal markings.
