29.17. Chord Symbols in Keyboard Music

29.17.1. Irregularly Spaced Chord Symbols

When the placement of chords is irregular, it is permissible to align the chords below the notes with which they coincide, inserting spaces into the lowest music line if necessary, in the manner employed for an instrumental lead sheet.
