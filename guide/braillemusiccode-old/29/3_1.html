Music Braille Code (2015)

29. Keyboard Instruments

29.3. Typical Structure of the Keyboard Music Parallel

29.3.1. Dividing a Measure between Parallels

A measure that cannot be completed in the parallel may be interrupted. (See Par. 28.1.1.) The music hyphen or measure- division sign is placed as the last sign of the line in each part. The measure number at the margin of the continuing parallel is followed by dot 3 to show that the measure is incomplete, and no space is left between the dot 3 and the right-hand sign. (See also Par. 29.3.5 regarding measure division at a long expression.)
