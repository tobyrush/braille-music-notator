Music Braille Code (2015)

35. Solo Songs and Choral Parts

35.12. Vocal Compass and Transposed Key

If the original key of a transposed part and/or the compass of the voice have been shown in the print and are to be indicated in the braille, this information should be given beginning at the margin on a separate line before the blank line that must precede the music heading. The compass is shown in music notation, with the lowest note (preceded by the music code indicator) followed immediately by the highest, closed by a double bar.
