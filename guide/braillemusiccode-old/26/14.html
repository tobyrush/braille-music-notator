Music Braille Code (2015)

26. Fretted Plucked String Instruments

26.14. TAB Notation

Music for guitar or ukulele is frequently printed in a system called TAB (a shortening of the word "tablature"). This notation cannot be directly represented in music braille. If a transcription is required, the TAB must first be translated accurately and completely into staff notation by a qualified reader of TAB.
