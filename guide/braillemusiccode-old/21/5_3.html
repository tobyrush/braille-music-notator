Music Braille Code (2015)

21. Annotations and Variants

21.5. Variant Readings

21.5.3. Variants Shown with the Variant Indicator

A variant, or a group of variants of the same original, may be written immediately after the original passage. The sign 5 is placed immediately before and immediately after each variant. When there are two or more variants, each is appropriately numbered, and if the passage contains more than one measure, the number of measures is placed after the first 5 before each variant. Use of this method is particularly appropriate when there are two or more concurrent fairly lengthy variants so that each variant may occupy a separate braille line.
