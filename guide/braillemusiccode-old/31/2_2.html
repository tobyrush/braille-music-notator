31. Harps

31.2. Pitch Settings

31.2.2. Changes of Pitch with Pedal Harps

In music for pedal harps, all of the notes with the same letter name in all octaves are changed at once, by adjusting the appropriate pedal. Changes of pitch are therefore often given simply by writing the name of a note with an accidental. The indication should be located as near as possible to its place in print. However, care must be taken to avoid locating the pedal change before the last occurrence of a pitch, in any octave, of the name that is to be changed.
