Music Braille Code (2015)

23. Chord Symbols

23.1. Transcribing Chord Symbols

23.1.1. Letters and Numbers

All letters and numbers are transcribed in uncontracted braille according to the literary code, except that the sign .#g represents the italic 7 for a specialized seventh chord. Grade 1 indicators are not employed. Numeric indicators are used. Periods are omitted. Capitalization follows the print, but passage capitalization is never used.
