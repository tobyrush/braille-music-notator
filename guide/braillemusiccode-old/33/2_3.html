Music Braille Code (2015)

33. Instrumental Ensemble Scores

33.2. List of Instruments

33.2.3. Instruments with Multiple Staves

When a score includes an instrument such as a piano, organ, or harp, which occupies two or more staves and therefore requires two or more lines of a parallel, each hand part is identified as if it were a separate instrument and the usual keyboard rules do not apply. (See Par. 33.4.2 and Example 33.7-1.) In the music, hand signs may be shown but are not required unless there are changes of hands.
