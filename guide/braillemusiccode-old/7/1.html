Music Braille Code (2015)

7. Meter Indications and Time Devices

7.1. Meter Signatures

A meter signature (time signature) is preceded (unless it immediately follows a key signature) and followed by blank spaces. A change of meter is placed in the music line wherever it appears in print and preferably is brailled in the same line as the following note. The first note following a meter signature requires an octave mark. (See Par. 1.7 regarding meter signatures in music headings.)
