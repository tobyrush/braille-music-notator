Music Braille Code (2015)

10. The Tie

10.1. Single Ties

10.1.2. Restatement of Ties

In bar-over-bar or open-score format, all ties are restated at the beginning of a new parallel. In single-line instrumental music, restatement is required at the beginning of a new segment or a new braille page; it is unnecessary at the commencement of a run-over line, unless it constitutes the beginning of a braille page. Restatement is also required in all formats after a major interruption such as a longer word-sign expression, a light double bar, a volta, etc. Reminder ties precede all other signs, except marginal measure numbers, hand signs, clefs, strain repeats, and time or key signatures. (See Par. 35.3.2 regarding restatement of the tie in vocal music.) Restatement of a chord tie (See Par. 10.2) is subject to the same rules.
