Music Braille Code (2015)

13. The Slur

13.9. Slurs with Appoggiaturas

13.9.1. Appoggiatura Slurs in Facsimile

In a facsimile transcription, the special slur ;C is employed for the short independent slurs. If four or more successive appoggiaturas occur, this sign is doubled by restating its second cell; bracket slurs may not be employed for this purpose in facsimile.
