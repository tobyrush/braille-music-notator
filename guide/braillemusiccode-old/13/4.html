Music Braille Code (2015)

13. The Slur

13.4. Convergence of Slurs

Sometimes one slur ends and another begins on the same note. When both phrases are short, a special slur sign ,C is used following the note that precedes the common note. Example 13.4-1 illustrates use of this special slur sign. When the slurs are both shown with bracket slurs, a four-cell sign consisting of an opening bracket followed by a closing bracket is placed before the common note. Example 13.4-2 illustrates such a concurrence of slurs, shown with (a) single and doubled slurs, and (b) bracket slurs.
