34.2. Typical Braille Transcription

34.2.1. Note Names

Music for pitched instruments is transcribed the same as keyboard music. When the music for unpitched instruments is printed in a five-line staff, the letter names of notes are used as if they were written in bass (F) clef. When it is printed on single or multiple horizontal lines, any notes may be used; the note C is the one most commonly used.
