Music Braille Code (2015)

24. Instrumental Solos and Ensemble Parts

24.3. Markings Extracted from a Score

When the transcription is extracted from a score in which the solo or individual part has been printed as part of a multi-staff score, the transcriber must take care to include any markings that may appear in the accompaniment or other places in the score when they apply to the music of the part being transcribed.
