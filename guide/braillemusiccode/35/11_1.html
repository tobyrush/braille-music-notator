Music Braille Code (2015)

35. Solo Songs and Choral Parts

35.11. Chants, Canticles, and Recitatives

35.11.1. Chant or Canticle Text Printed without Music

When the text of a chant is printed without including the notes, it is transcribed in paragraph form, each verse being given as a paragraph introduced by its number enclosed between literary parentheses. Pointing symbols and asterisks are given as described in Par. 35.11.
