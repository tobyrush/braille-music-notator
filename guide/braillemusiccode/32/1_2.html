Music Braille Code (2015)

32. Accordions

32.1. Accordion Notation

32.1.2. Chord Buttons Shown as Intervals

When notes for chords stand over basses of identical time value, being played simultaneously with them, these chord notes may be written as intervals preceded by the appropriate chord signs. In this case each chord sign applies to one interval only. Intervals may be doubled, but the doubling must be broken before a change of row.
