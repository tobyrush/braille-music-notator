Music Braille Code (2015)

1. General Procedures and Signs

1.6. The Page Heading

On all braille pages other than the title page and braille page 1, a running title is centered between the two page indications. If, after all efforts to condense it, the running title, with at least three blank spaces on each side, cannot be fitted between the page numbers, the inclusive print pagination is moved to the beginning of the second line. The remainder of that line must be left blank.
