Music Braille Code (2015)

1. General Procedures and Signs

1.5. Pagination of Music Pages

1.5.2. Print Page Turnover Indication

The turnover sign, the same sign as the pagination indicator, should be written between spaces where the page turn occurs in the music. If the braille page encompasses portions of more than two print pages, the turnover sign should be followed immediately by the appropriate page number at the point of each page change. In keyboard or other multilineal format, the turnover must be shown in each music line. In line-by-line vocal music, each turnover (always including prefix and page number) should appear only in the music line(s). Print pagination and turnovers should be shown in the accompaniment as well. The first note after a turnover indication requires an octave mark.
