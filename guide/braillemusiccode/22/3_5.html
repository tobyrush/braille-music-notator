Music Braille Code (2015)

22. Nuances and Verbal Expressions

22.3. Words and Abbreviated Words of Expression

22.3.5. Expressions Related to the Preceding Notes

Most word-sign expressions refer to the music that follows them. Occasionally, a word or other expression applies to the end of the previous note. In that case, the expression is placed immediately following the note and any fingering, interval, slur, or tie that follows the note.
