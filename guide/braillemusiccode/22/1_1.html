Music Braille Code (2015)

22. Nuances and Verbal Expressions

22.1. Symbols That Precede the Note in Braille

22.1.1. Doubling Symbols of Expression and Articulation

Any of these signs except the swell sign may be doubled if it applies to four or more successive notes. All doubling of signs in effect at the end of a braille page should be restated at the beginning of a new page, assuming that at each instance there is a sufficient number of notes to warrant such doubling. Thus, doubling will not be started at the bottom of a page if there are not at least three consecutive similar signs on that page, nor will the doubling be restated at the top of the new page if there are not at least four of them at the beginning of the page.
