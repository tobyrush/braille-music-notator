Music Braille Code (2015)

22. Nuances and Verbal Expressions

22.3. Words and Abbreviated Words of Expression

22.3.2. Abbreviated Words

Abbreviated words are placed without any intervening spaces at the appropriate points in the music text before or after the relevant notes. Various print abbreviations may appear for the words "crescendo," "decrescendo," and "diminuendo." The abbreviations >cr', >decr', and >dim' are employed regardless of how the words may be abbreviated in print. An abbreviation, such as "L.V." or "lv" for the phrase "let vibrate," in any language, is represented by the abbreviation >lvb' regardless of how it may be given in print. All other abbreviations are transcribed following the print.
