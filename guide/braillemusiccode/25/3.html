Music Braille Code (2015)

25. Bowed String Instruments

25.3. Bowing Marks

A bowing sign should be placed as near to the note as possible and should not be separated from it except by the signs for ornaments, nuances, accidentals, and octaves. An opening bracket slur should precede the bowing sign. The note does not require a special octave mark. A bowing sign may be doubled if it applies to four or more successive notes; the entire two-cell sign must be restated.
