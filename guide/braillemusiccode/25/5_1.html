Music Braille Code (2015)

25. Bowed String Instruments

25.5. Arco and Pizzicato

25.5.1. Left-hand Pizzicato

Pizzicato for the left hand is indicated by the sign _> placed before each note so played. A dot 3 must follow this sign if the next sign contains dot 1, 2, or 3. The next note requires a special octave mark. This sign may be doubled in a passage of four or more notes. The technique is frequently indicated in print by a cross above or below the note. In braille, the print "pizz.," and such indications as "m.g." or "LH," are omitted from the left-hand pizzicato notes or chords except in a facsimile transcription.
