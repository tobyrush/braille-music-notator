Music Braille Code (2015)

12. Stem Signs

12.1. Function of Stem Signs

It is sometimes difficult or impossible to render concisely, by means of the in-accord sign, passages containing notes printed with two stems indicating either identical or differing values. The stem signs are used for this purpose. Stem signs should be used with great discretion; the in-accord device is preferable where it can be used. The values of notes shown in print with additional stems may sometimes not be values that are rational in the meter of the measure. In such cases the use of stem signs is the only means by which the print can be represented in braille.
