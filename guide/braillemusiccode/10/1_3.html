Music Braille Code (2015)

10. The Tie

10.1. Single Ties

10.1.3. Restatement of Accidentals with Tied Notes

When a note inflected by an accidental is tied over a bar line, the accidental is restated if it is re-marked in the print. It is also restated, preceded by dot 5, when the accidental is not re- marked in print and the new measure is at the beginning of a new braille line or parallel, or if it follows a major interruption. The accidental is not restated when the second tied note falls at the beginning of a run-over line when the run-over line is a continuation of a divided measure, unless it would have been restated had the measure continued on the same line.
