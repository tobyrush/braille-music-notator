Music Braille Code (2015)

10. The Tie

10.1. Single Ties

The tie is placed immediately after the first of the two tied notes, or after any dot, fingering, tremolo indication, and/or slur. (See also Par. 13.5 regarding ties and slurs together. See Pars. 18.1.2, 18.2.1, and 19.3 regarding ties in relation to braille repeat devices.)
