Music Braille Code (2015)

43. Note-for-Note

43.1. Note-for-Note Method

43.1.6. The Moving Note Sign

The moving-note sign may be used in note-for-note for very obvious cases. The double moving-note sign, however, must not be used in this method. If the moving-note sign is extensively used, the octave rules for subnotes are those that apply to written notes.
