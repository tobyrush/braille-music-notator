Music Braille Code (2015)

43. Note-for-Note

43.2. Changes of Meaning of Music Braille Signs

43.2.2. Substituting the Unused Interval Signs

The interval signs / + # - and 3 which are displaced by the note-for-note method may be used as follows:

Acciaccatura
Appoggiatura
Turn between notes
Turn above a note
Repeat replacing 7
Irregular grouping replacing _
Trill and mordents
Staccatos, accents, etc.
