Music Braille Code (2015)

43. Note-for-Note

43.1. Note-for-Note Method

43.1.1. Direction of Reading

The direction in which subnotes are read (upward or downward) depends very much on the instrument for which the music is written and on the disposition of the score. Where clef signs are used, the treble clef implies a downward reading and the bass clef an upward reading.
