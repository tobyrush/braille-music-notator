Music Braille Code (2015)

43. Note-for-Note

43.2. Changes of Meaning of Music Braille Signs

43.2.1. Separating These Signs with the Hyphen Sign

These signs may be separated from the preceding music text by the sign - which is not otherwise used in note-for-note except (a) at the beginning of a measure or of a braille line, (b) after a rest, piano pedaling, hand or foot signs, or (c) after marks of expression of the type given in Table 22(C).
