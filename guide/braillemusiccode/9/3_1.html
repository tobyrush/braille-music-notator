Music Braille Code (2015)

9. Intervals

9.3. Doubling of Intervals

9.3.1. Restatement of Interval Doublings

All doubling of intervals in effect at the end of a braille page should be restated at the beginning of a new page, assuming that at each instance there is a sufficient number of notes to warrant such doubling. Thus, doubling will not be started at the bottom of a page if there are not at least three consecutive similar intervals on that page, nor will the doubling be restated at the top of the new page if there are not at least four of them at the beginning of the page.
