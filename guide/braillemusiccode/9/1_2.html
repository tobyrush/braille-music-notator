Music Braille Code (2015)

9. Intervals

9.1. Transcribing Chords

9.1.2. Crossed Voices in Successive Chords

When two parts represented by intervals cross one another in a chord, each note that is “out of place” must have its proper octave mark, the written order of the parts however remaining unchanged. It may be preferable to show crossed voices by means of an in-accord. (See Sec. 11.)
