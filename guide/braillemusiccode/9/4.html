Music Braille Code (2015)

9. Intervals

9.4. Tone Clusters

Tone clusters are printed in many different ways. Usually the top and bottom pitches are written as if they are pitches in a chord. Between the two pitches, a vertical line, box, thick bar, or other shape indicates the cluster. Accidentals or text may or may not accompany the cluster. In braille, the top or bottom pitch is written, according to the appropriate direction of intervals, followed by the cluster sign and an interval sign indicating the other outside pitch.
