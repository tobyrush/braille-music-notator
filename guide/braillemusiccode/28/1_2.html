28. Instrumental Bar-Over-Bar Format

28.1. General Principles

28.1.2. Run-Over Lines

When the amount of music in one line of a parallel that contains only one measure is significantly greater than the other(s), the parallel may be expanded. The longer line is divided into two or more successive lines. A run-over line is indented two spaces beyond the initial alignment of the other lines. It is not generally advisable to extend more than one line of a parallel.
