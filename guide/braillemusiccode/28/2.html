Music Braille Code (2015)

28. Instrumental Bar-Over-Bar Format

28.2. Repeat Devices

Measure-number repeats are used liberally. A backward-numeral repeat may only be used when the original passage and its repetition occur in the same parallel. Part-measure repeats are used freely, except that a part-measure repeat may never be used as the continuation of a measure that is divided between lines or parallels. The braille da capo and dal segno devices may be employed for extensive repetitions when all details of the affected passages are identical.
