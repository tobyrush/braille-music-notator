Music Braille Code (2015)

39. Line-over-Line

39.3. Unmeasured Passages

If within a piece there is an unmeasured portion that is clearly neither a cadenza nor ad libitum, such passage should be identified as "unmeasured" and resumption of measured music identified by the word "measured." Coincidence signs or beat-over-beat could also be employed. In any case, a transcriber's note will be needed to explain the unusual format.
