Music Braille Code (2015)

39. Line-over-Line

39.2. Stave Numbering

In the transcription of unmeasured music, marginal numbering can be geared to stave numbering. Line-over-line format may be used, numbering each parallel according to staves. For the sake of clarity, it may be necessary to use either coincidence signs or beat-over-beat. (See Par. 29.5.)
