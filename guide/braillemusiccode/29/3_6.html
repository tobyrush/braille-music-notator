29. Keyboard Instruments

29.3. Typical Structure of the Keyboard Music Parallel

29.3.6. Dividing an In-Accord Measure between Parallels

When a measure in which the in-accord device is employed must be divided between successive parallels, care must be taken to assure that the same rhythmic portion of the measure appears in both hand parts in each parallel. When the last sign of a line is a measure-division sign, it is not necessary to add a music hyphen.
