29. Keyboard Instruments

29.3. Typical Structure of the Keyboard Music Parallel

29.3.2. Run-Over Line Used When Measure Cannot Be Divided

Sometimes one part in a measure that starts at the beginning of the parallel is longer than the available space in the line, but the measure cannot be divided at the same point in both lines. In that case, it is necessary to employ one or more run-over lines. (See Par. 28.1.2.)
