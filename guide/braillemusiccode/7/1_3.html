Music Braille Code (2015)

7. Meter Indications and Time Devices

7.1. Meter Signatures

7.1.3. Combined Meter Signatures

When meter signatures are combined, the two signatures are brailled without an intervening space. Each of the component signatures begins with a numeric indicator. Any punctuation or other print signs are placed as they occur in the print. Parentheses are represented by the music signs, not by the literary ones.
