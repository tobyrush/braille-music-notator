Music Braille Code (2015)

23. Chord Symbols

23.3. Chord Symbols in Various Formats

Instructions for transcribing chord symbols with single-staff music without lyrics are given in Sec. 27. Instructions for single-staff music with lyrics are given in Sec. 36. Instructions for music in instrumental bar-over-bar formats are given in Pars. 29.17–29.17.1.
