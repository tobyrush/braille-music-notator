Music Braille Code (2015)

26. Fretted Plucked String Instruments

26.9. Barré

26.9.1. Barré Indicated by Letters and/or Fractions

When a barré is indicated in print with letters and/or fractions, the letters or fractions are not given in the braille, except in the case of a facsimile transcription. Instead, the sign for full barré _ or for partial barré ^ immediately precedes the position sign.
