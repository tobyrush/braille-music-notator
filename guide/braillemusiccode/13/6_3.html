13. The Slur

13.6. Slurs between Parts

13.6.3. Straight Line between Staves

The transfer of a melodic line from one staff to another is sometimes indicated in print by a straight line from a note in the first staff to a note in the second staff. The straight line is represented by the sign @L following the first of the two notes. If there is any doubt about which of two or more in-accord parts may be the intended continuation of the phrase, the sign for the end of a straight line between staves .@L is placed at the beginning of the continuation.
