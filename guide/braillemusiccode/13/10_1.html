Music Braille Code (2015)

13. The Slur

13.10. Unattached Slurs

13.10.1. Slurs That Do Not Come from Notes

The sign .;C is used to show a slur that is not initiated from a note. It is placed immediately before the note to which it leads, and before any associated nuance, accidental, or octave mark. The sign may not be doubled.
