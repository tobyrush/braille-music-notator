Music Braille Code (2015)

41. Vertical Score and Bar-by-Bar

41.3. Bar-by-Bar

41.3.1. Bar Line

The bar line L is placed between the highest part in one measure and the lowest part in the following measure, and it has a free space on either side. The bar line follows every measure of a piece (even where the print has double bars with or without dots) except the final one.
