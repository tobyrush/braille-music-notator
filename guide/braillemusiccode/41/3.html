Music Braille Code (2015)

41. Vertical Score and Bar-by-Bar

41.3. Bar-by-Bar

This method is only used for two- or three-stave instrumental scores, such as piano and organ music, or piano combined with the outline of a solo voice or instrumental part. The score is laid out horizontally, a measure of one part being followed after an intervening space by the corresponding measure in another part, and so on. The order of the parts is from the lowest upward, and intervals and in-accords are also read upward.
