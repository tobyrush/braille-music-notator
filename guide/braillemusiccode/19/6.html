Music Braille Code (2015)

19. Braille Numeral Repeats

19.6. Signs That May Be Attached to a Numeral Repeat

Braille numeral repeats apply to the contents of measures, not added signs such as double bars, voltas, and print repeat signs. A double bar, a final double bar, or a print end-of-repeat sign may be placed without spacing immediately following a braille numeral repeat marking.
