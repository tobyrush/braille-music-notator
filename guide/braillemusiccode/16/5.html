Music Braille Code (2015)

16. Ornaments

16.5. Mordents

The sign for any of the four types of mordents is brailled before the affected note or interval. If a mordent is shown affecting a note represented by an interval sign, the braille mordent sign is placed before the interval sign. An accidental that modifies the mordent, either above or below it, is brailled before the mordent sign. A fingering is brailled after the note or interval.
