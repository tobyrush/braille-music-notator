16.7. Ornaments Derived from Jazz Idioms

The meanings for performance of the jazz-derived signs given in Table 16(E) are not standardized. They must be transcribed according to their appearance, rather than their intent. The sign is placed before or after the note, following print; a sign printed directly above or below the note is brailled before the note. If these signs affect notes that are represented as intervals in chords, the sign must be given with each affected note and/or interval sign.
