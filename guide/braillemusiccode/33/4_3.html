Music Braille Code (2015)

33. Instrumental Ensemble Scores

33.4. The Parallel

33.4.3. Braille Repeats

Very obvious measure or part-measure repeats may be used when they occur on the same braille line as the original passage. Braille numeral repeats may not be used. The braille da capo and dal segno devices may be employed for extensive repetitions when all details of the affected passages are identical.
