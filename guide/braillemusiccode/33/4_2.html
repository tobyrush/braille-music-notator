33.4.2. Intervals and In-Accords

Intervals and in-accords are read upward in all parts. The braille interval signs should be used freely, except in divisi parts for string instruments. In string music, braille intervals must be reserved for double, triple or quadruple stops; the only exception to this restriction is a divisi passage in octaves.
