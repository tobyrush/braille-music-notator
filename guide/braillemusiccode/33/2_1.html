33.2.1. Abbreviations of Instrument Names

A list of abbreviations for the English, French, Italian, and German names of the usual orchestral instruments is given in Table 29. English names and abbreviations are used in all of the following examples. It will often be necessary for the transcriber to devise abbreviations for instruments that are not included in Table 29, and these should always be limited to two or three letters conveying an immediate suggestion of the name (e.g., glo for glockenspiel, tt for tam-tam, etc.).
