30. Figured Bass and Harmonic Analysis

30.5. Analytical Symbols in Literary Text

When roman numerals as analytical symbols occur in a literary text, they may be transcribed as ordinary roman numerals. However, when they are combined with arabic numerals or other signs, the combined symbols should be shown as music signs, employing the opening music code indicator ,' and terminator ;2 as described in Par. 1.16.
