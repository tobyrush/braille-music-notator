30.4. Harmonic Analysis

30.4.2. Using Open Score for Harmonic Analysis

In complex excerpts, such as those that would involve in-accords, an open-score presentation is preferable. Arabic numerals and accidentals are brailled in vertical columns if they are printed vertically. An appropriate number of dots 36 are used to represent any horizontal lines printed between inversions.
