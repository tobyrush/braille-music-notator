30.2.7. Bracketed and Parenthetical Figures

Parentheses that appear in print are represented by music parentheses. When small brackets enclose a feature, the special signs for small brackets must be used in facsimile copy; in nonfacsimile copy, music parentheses may be used. (See Par. 1.13.2.)
