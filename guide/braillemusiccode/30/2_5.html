30.2. Figured Bass in Horizontal Mode

30.2.5. Modifier Signs with Figured Bass

Signs that may sometimes occur in a passage of figured bass include triplets, notes in large type, turns, short appoggiaturas, trills, mordents, staccatos, accents, and repeats. There is no possibility of confusion unless one of these signs immediately follows a figure or column of figures in the same braille line, in which case it must be preceded by the sign - as a separator.
