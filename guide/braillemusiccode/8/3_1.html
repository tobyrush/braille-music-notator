8. Grouping of Notes

8.3. Irregular Beams Shown by Music Comma

8.3.1. Irregular Beaming of Unequal Notes

The music comma may also be used to indicate special beaming of groups of notes of unequal values. Note grouping should not be employed where special beaming suggests a meter in conflict with the given one.
