Music Braille Code (2015)

27. Instrumental Lead Sheets

27.6. Strumming Signs

Oblique lines used to indicate rhythmic strumming are occasionally shown with a melody without words. Strumming signs are represented by dots 34 and are placed on a separate line directly below the notes to which they apply. If chord symbols also appear, they are placed on the bottom line of a three-line parallel, aligned with the strumming signs with which they are associated.
