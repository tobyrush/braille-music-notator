Music Braille Code (2015)

11. In-Accord and Measure-Division Signs

11.2. Restating Accidentals in In-Accords

Accidentals brailled before a measure division sign or either type of in-accord sign do not affect notes in the same measure that are brailled after the sign. It is therefore often necessary to re- mark accidentals to avoid possible errors in reading. Accidentals added by the transcriber must be preceded by dot 5.
