Music Braille Code (2015)

6. Accidentals and Key Signatures

6.1. Placement of Accidentals

An accidental is placed immediately before the note or interval to which it belongs and must not be separated from it by anything but an octave mark. In general, accidentals should be marked wherever they occur in print. When an accidental has been printed above or below its note rather than in the staff, the dot 6 prefix should immediately precede the sign.
