Music Braille Code (2015)

6. Accidentals and Key Signatures

6.2. Restating Accidentals

As in print, an accidental remains in force until it is countermanded or until the end of the measure, but only for the same note at the same pitch. An accidental should be restated at the beginning of a segment or parallel, or a new braille page, or after any major interruption such as a long word-sign expression, a volta, etc. Every transcriber-added accidental must be preceded by dot 5.
