Music Braille Code (2015)

36. Chord Symbols with Lyrics

36.3. Alignment of Chord Symbols below Lyrics

36.3.4. Chord Sounded following End of Syllable

Where a chord is sounded after the syllable or word has been released, the initial capital sign of the chord is placed one space to the right in the line below the final letter. If previous chordal material already extends beyond the word, the chord follows this material without space, unless the previous chord contains two capital letters that are the names of notes.
