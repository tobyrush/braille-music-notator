Music Braille Code (2015)

36. Chord Symbols with Lyrics

36.5. Chord Changes in Strophic Songs

36.5.1. Minor Variations of Text between Two Verses

Where a second verse has only a few words different from the first verse, the variation may be shown within the first verse provided the original and the variation can be shown in one braille line. The indication of verse number and the variation are enclosed in word signs. The chords are shown under each version, but the melody is written only once.
