Music Braille Code (2015)

40. Section-by-Section

40.1. Format of a Section

40.1.1. Section Headings

A free line must be left above the first line of each section. In this line the serial number of the section, the numbers of the measures contained in that section, and the numbers of the print page and stave (or staves) for that section must be centered in this order. The measure numbers are written in the lower part of the cell and are separated from each other by dots 36; the page and stave numbers are written in the upper part of the cell preceded by the sign "3 and are separated from each other by dot 3. If more than one stave is indicated, the sign - separates the stave numbers from each other. If a section commences or concludes with an incomplete measure, the measure numbers in the heading must be followed by dot 3 without an intervening space, and if the first section commences with an incomplete measure, the braille numeral 0, written in the lower part of the cell, replaces the number.
