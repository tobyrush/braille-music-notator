Music Braille Code (2015)

3. Octave Signs

3.3. 8va and 15ma

In nonfacsimile transcriptions, the words "8va," "15ma," "loco," and similar expressions are represented by transcribing the pitches in the octave in which they are to be performed without noting the expressions. In facsimile copy, these indications may be transcribed using either of two methods.
