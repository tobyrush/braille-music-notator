18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.7. Sequence Abbreviation

18.7.2. Sequence Abbreviation in a Facsimile Transcription

In a facsimile transcription, the sequence abbreviation sign is modified by adding dot 3 (dots 36, 3), to inform the reader that the sequence device was not used in the print.
