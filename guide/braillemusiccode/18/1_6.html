Music Braille Code (2015)

18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.1. General Use of the Braille Repeat Sign

18.1.6. Signs That May Be Attached to the Repeat Sign

Braille repeats apply to the contents of a measure, not added signs such as double bars, voltas, and print repeat signs. A double bar, final double bar, print end-of-repeat sign, or a breath or break sign may be placed without spacing immediately following a braille repeat sign.
