Music Braille Code (2015)

18. Braille Full- and Part-Measure Repeats, Parallel Movement, and Sequence Abbreviation

18.2. Full-Measure Repeats

18.2.2. Full-Measure Repeats in Bar-over-Bar Format

In bar-over-bar format, a full-measure repeat may be used in any of the braille music lines. When a measure is repeated in all lines, the repeat signs must be vertically aligned. A full-measure repeat may be employed in the first measure of a parallel, but not at the beginning of a new section or braille page.
